My Water

Increase or decrease your daily water goal by going to Settings -> Health Goals (scroll the top tab bar). Look for the Water field to make changes. Each cup of water is 8 ounces (237 mL).

My Steps

Get movin'! Premium members can connect a fitness device to automatically import and track steps. To manually add steps, tap the + button above. To edit these steps, go to My Day and select the Quick Entry associated with these steps.
