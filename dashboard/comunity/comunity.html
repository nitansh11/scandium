Earn points and compete with other members. Get motivated, stay a countable, and crush it with this challenge!

Here are the rules. Each Monday, a new challenge starts and runs for 4 weeks. You can join or leave the current challenge at any point.

Earn points by logging your foods, keeping under your carb goal, hitting your macros, drinking enough water, and exercising. The top individual scorers will appear in a leaderboard. Connect with other participants to get motivated. The maximum points per day is 130.

While we calculate your scores automatically based on what you log, there is an honor system component to this challenge. Please play fair and be honest to see your best results!
